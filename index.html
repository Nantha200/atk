our services
Exploring the Role of Textiles

ATK Group provides complete textile solutions — from ginning and spinning to dyeing, printing, stitching, and finishing — ensuring unmatched quality across every step.

Ginning Division

Ethically sourced cotton is cleaned and processed using advanced ginning machinery, forming the base for premium fabric production.

Spinning & Weaving

Cotton fibers are spun into yarn and woven into uniform fabric with precision, using high-speed automated looms.

Dyeing & Printing

Eco-friendly dyeing and digital printing techniques bring vibrant, long-lasting color to textiles for global brands.

Cutting & Stitching

Customized apparel and home textiles are cut and stitched to specification using computer-aided precision tools.

Embroidery & Finishing

Premium final touches include decorative embroidery, steam ironing, and safe packing for domestic and export delivery.

About Us
Shaping the Future of Textiles Since 1993

Established in 1993, ATK Group is a vertically integrated textile manufacturer based in Theni, India. We deliver high-quality, sustainable fabrics to the home, hospitality, and healthcare industries worldwide.

01

Vertically Integrated Manufacturing

From ginning to finishing, ATK Mills operates a fully integrated facility in Theni, delivering quality at every stage of the textile production process.

02

Global Export & Presence

With a strong global footprint and logistics network, ATK Mills exports high-quality fabrics and textile products to clients around the world.

03

Dedicated Client Support

We work closely with clients to deliver tailored solutions, backed by our 5000+ skilled professionals and a commitment to quality and service excellence.
