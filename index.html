Ginning Division

Ethically sourced cotton is cleaned and processed using advanced ginning machinery, forming the base for premium fabric production.

Spinning

We spin fibers into high-grade yarns using Compact, Open-End, and Vortex Airjet technologies in our in-house facility.

Weaving
