our Services
We provide best solution, Take a Look Into Our Best Services
Cotton Ginning

We begin at the root — sourcing cotton directly from ethical farms. Our ginning unit blends tradition with innovation to clean, sort, and bale raw cotton.

Spinning

We spin fibers into high-grade yarns using Compact, Open-End, and Vortex Airjet technologies in our in-house facility.

Sizing & Warping

Our advanced Karl Mayer systems ensure that threads are perfectly prepared for weaving, minimizing breakage and ensuring efficiency.

Weaving

We weave stories into fabric using Air-jet, Rapier, and Projectile Looms — producing textiles for luxury and commercial applications.

Dyeing & Finishing

Color comes to life here. With Osthoff Singeing, CBR, Mercerizing, and CDR machines, we ensure vibrant, lasting dyes.

Printing

Your designs deserve precision. Our Rotary and Digital printing units deliver vibrant prints with superior clarity and consistency.

Quilting & Embroidery

Our quilting and embroidery units use multi-needle and automated embroidery machines to create detailed, luxurious pieces.

Cutting, Stitching & Washing

Precision cutting and stitching turn fabric into function — tailored to your specifications.

Quality Control

Nothing leaves ATK without thorough testing. Our lab ensures compliance, durability, and aesthetic excellence.

Sustainable Manufacturing

With a Zero Liquid Discharge (ZLD) facility and 80% renewable energy usage, we put the planet first.

ATK Group

We are a vertically integrated textile manufacturer, delivering quality from cotton to final fabric for home, healthcare, and hospitality sectors worldwide.
